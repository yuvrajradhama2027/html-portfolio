Must Watch Animes According To Yuvraj

The top 3 in the list are-:

1- Naruto - Naruto Shippuden

A story filled with a lot of goosebumps, a story which will touch your heart a connection which you'll make with the main character which you'll never forget.This has the amalgamation of everything you want to feel.

2- Attack on titan

Heart breaking and can be a bit edgy for some viewers otherwise everything is worth it, full of mysteries , surprises, weird time logic and an emotional roller coaster ride. And warning once you'll start watching it you can't stop yourself from binge watching it.

3- Aaoshi

Just a lovely story with beautiful sceneries of japan and an underdog soccer player from a weak financial background ,trying to make it in the big league, but he has something special about him,watch this to know what.
